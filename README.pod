=head1 NAME

lgold - Liquid Gold

=head1 SYNOPSIS

Visualization for the Bill Lane Center for the West, Stanford University.
Fall 2010 - Liquid Gold.

=head1 DESCRIPTION

Liquid Gold or lgold tries to visualize the location/size of water reservoirs in California in the historical context of population growth in the state.
